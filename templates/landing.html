Our Journey

Milestones in our mission to revolutionize Indian agriculture

January 2019

Company Foundation

Robic Rufarm Private Limited

  • Established Robic Rufarm with a vision to revolutionize Indian agriculture through technology
  • Assembled a team of experts in agronomy, engineering, and data science
  • Conducted initial market research to identify key pain points for rural farmers
  • Developed the company's mission statement and core objectives
March 2020 - June 2020

First AI Prototype

Crop Monitoring System

  • Designed and developed our first AI prototype for crop health monitoring
  • Created models to analyze satellite imagery for early detection of pests and diseases
  • Built a simple mobile app interface for farmers with multilingual support
  • Tested the system with three local farming communities with promising results
January 2021 - April 2021

Product Launch

FarmSense Platform

  • Officially launched our flagship FarmSense platform for comprehensive farm monitoring
  • Integrated new features including automated alerts, yield predictions, and advisory services
  • Partnered with 15 medium-sized farming communities across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh
  • Achieved a 25% increase in productivity for early adopters
January 2023 - Present

Expansion & Innovation

Scaling Across Rural India

  • Expanded operations to serve over 5,000 farmers across 20+ villages
  • Introduced the new SmartIrrigation system for precision water management
  • Secured strategic partnerships with agricultural universities for ongoing research
  • Developed farmer-specific analytics to maximize yield while minimizing resource use
